## Olist join graph (required order)

- olist_orders_raw → olist_order_items_raw on order_id
- olist_order_items_raw → olist_sellers_raw on seller_id (required for seller_state / seller_city — orders has no seller_id)
- olist_order_items_raw → olist_products_raw on product_id
- olist_products_raw → olist_category_translation_raw on product_category_name (left join)
- olist_orders_raw → olist_customers_raw on customer_id (required for customer_state)

Never join category_translation before products — product_category_name exists only on products.

Use aliases: orders=o, items=i, products=p, translation=t, customers=c, sellers=s.
Example chain:
